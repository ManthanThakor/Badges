Virat Kohli

Profile Summary

Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer and former captain of the Indian national team. He is regarded as one of the best batsmen in the world.

Achievements

  • ICC Cricketer of the Year: 2017, 2018
  • ICC ODI Player of the Year: 2012, 2017, 2018
  • Leading run-scorer in T20 Internationals

Conclusion

Virat Kohli's dedication and talent have made him a global cricket icon. His achievements on the field continue to inspire millions.
